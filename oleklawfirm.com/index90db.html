About Our Firm.

Oleksandr Radchenko began his legal career after graduating from the Academy of Advocacy of Ukraine in 1997 with a Specialist Degree in Law. He spent a decade under Mikhail Shevchenko, where he refined his expertise in international intellectual property disputes.
Following his mentor’s passing in 2020, Oleksandr established his own firm, continuing the principles of precision and integrity he had learned. Over the years, his practice has grown into a respected team of professionals, serving clients worldwide and upholding a reputation for excellence in intellectual property protection and legal strategy.

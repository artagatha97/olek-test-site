About Us

Our Story

Founded by Oleksandr Radchenko, our firm is built on decades of experience, integrity, and a dedication to protecting innovation across borders. After completing the Academy of Advocacy of Ukraine with a Specialist Degree in Law in 1997, Oleksandr began his career at the guidance of Mikhail Shevchenko, where he spent ten years specializing in international intellectual property disputes. 
Following the passing of his mentor in 2020, Oleksandr decided to continue that legacy by establishing his own independent practice. Today, the firm has grown into a respected team of ten professionals, providing partner-level service to clients ranging from individual creators to multinational corporations. Our focus lies in delivering precise, tailored legal solutions in the field of intellectual property, ensuring every client receives dedicated attention and strategic guidance. Although based in Ukraine, our firm serves clients worldwide, with a particular focus on intellectual property matters across the European Union, the United States, and China. 

We combine local insight with international perspective to protect and advance our clients’ rights in a complex global landscape. Our company and its members are actively engaged in 28 national and international non-governmental organizations, including:
AIPPI, INTA, PTMG, IBA, Ukrainian Patent Attorney’s Association (UPAA), ICC Ukraine, American Chamber of Commerce (ACC) in Ukraine, German-Ukrainian Chamber of Industry and Commerce (AHK Ukraine), European Business Association (EBA), Ukrainian Trademark Association (UTA), Ukrainian Bar Association (UBA), Ukrainian Advocates’ Association (UAA), among others.
In addition, the firm is an active member of the Ukraine Alliance Against Counterfeiting and Piracy (UAACP) — part of the Global Anti-Counterfeiting Group (GACG Network), which unites 24 national and regional organizations in the fight against counterfeit goods worldwide. Guided by professionalism, clarity, and respect for the creative process, we remain committed to protecting the ideas, brands, and innovations that define progress.
